How to Play

1. Click to Generate Cookies: The core mechanic of cookie clicker games involves clicking on a large cookie or a button to generate cookies. Each click produces a certain number of cookies, which serve as the game's currency.

2. Upgrade Buildings: As you accumulate cookies, you can use them to purchase various buildings and upgrades. These buildings include cookie factories, farms, mines, and more. Each building generates cookies automatically over time, even when you're not actively playing the

3. Unlock Upgrades: In addition to buildings, you can also unlock upgrades that enhance cookie production or provide other bonuses. These upgrades typically require a certain number of cookies to purchase.

4. Monitor Progress: Keep an eye on your cookie production rate and total cookie count. The goal is to continuously increase your production rate by purchasing more buildings and upgrades.

5. Idle Progress: Even when you're not actively clicking, your buildings will continue to generate cookies. This makes idle play an essential aspect of cookie clicker games. You can leave the game running in the background and come back later to collect your earnings and purchase more upgrades.

Remember, the gameplay is typically straightforward and revolves around the addictive cycle of clicking to generate cookies, using those cookies to buy buildings and upgrades, and watching your production grow exponentially over time. Enjoy baking those virtual cookies!
